

## Fitness Store — Full-Featured Shopify Storefront

### 1. Enable Shopify Integration
- Create a new Shopify development store through Lovable
- Import your product catalog (cycling jackets, ankle weights, jump ropes, cycling caps, dry bags, compression wear, gym shorts, yoga tanks, and more) from your CSV files
- Set up product variants (colors, sizes) and inventory levels

### 2. Homepage — Bold & Energetic Design
- **Hero section** with a dynamic full-width banner featuring a strong fitness-themed headline, call-to-action button, and bold typography
- **Featured products carousel** highlighting bestsellers or new arrivals
- **Category/collection grid** (e.g., Cycling Gear, Fitness Equipment, Activewear) with bold imagery
- **Why Shop With Us** section with value propositions (free shipping, quality guarantee, etc.)
- **Customer reviews/testimonials** section
- **Newsletter signup** section with email capture

### 3. Product Catalog & Collections Page
- Grid layout with product cards showing images, prices, and discount badges (compare-at prices)
- Filter and sort options (by category, price, color)
- Collection pages for product categories (Cycling, Fitness Equipment, Activewear, Accessories)

### 4. Product Detail Page
- Large product image gallery with variant-specific images
- Color and size selector with visual swatches
- Price display with original/sale price comparison
- Product description with collapsible detail sections
- Customer reviews display
- "Add to Cart" button with quantity selector
- Related products section

### 5. Shopping Cart & Checkout
- Slide-out cart drawer showing added items
- Quantity adjustment and item removal
- Cart summary with subtotal
- Checkout flow powered by Shopify Checkout

### 6. About Us Page
- Brand story and mission statement
- Bold, fitness-inspired imagery
- Team or brand values section

### 7. Contact Page
- Contact form (name, email, message)
- Store location info (Hay El Farah)
- Social media links

### 8. Global Elements
- **Header** with logo, navigation menu, search, and cart icon with item count
- **Footer** with quick links, social media, and newsletter signup
- **Mobile-responsive** design throughout
- **Bold & energetic color scheme** — dark backgrounds, vibrant accent colors (orange/red fitness tones), strong typography
- Smooth animations and hover effects for an engaging user experience
